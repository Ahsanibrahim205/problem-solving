Why problem solving is
valuable in improving your wellness

    “If you can’t get over under or around the wall then your way forward is blocked. Get yourself a ladder or a spade or a map, then you can move past your problem.’’

    • Problem solving techniques can help us to overcome obstacles along the way to achieving our goals and enjoying the sense of satisfaction and happiness that follows.
    • It is not only the big issues and problems that can lead to anxiety and stress. Small problems can be the immediate cause of anxiety and stress.
    • When we have too many things to do along combined with high standards to meet, we may worry about what to do.
    • This is when being a skilled problem solver can help you immensely.
    • When faced with coping with difficult situations that are causing you problems, you risk feeling helpless and losing hope of getting a successful outcome. Anxiety is ever close in these situations too.
    • Having a technique for problem solving will improve your coping capabilities
      1. identify and define problems
      2. prioritise problems
      3. choose one to work on
      4. come up with realistic solutions
      5. Re-assess and refine solutions.
    • Whenever you have a task to do that is problematic and find yourself thinking, “What if x occurs?” or “Now that x has occurred …” you will know how to generate the solution that you need.
    • This enables you to think more confidently and have thoughts like, “If x occurs, then I will z”, where z is the solution.

      • Overall this is called contingency planning or avoiding your worst nightmares
